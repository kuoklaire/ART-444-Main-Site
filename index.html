Hi! My name is Claire Kuo.

I am an User Interface and Graphic Designer with experience in the field of graphic design, marketing communications, and was previously a UI intern at BOX Digital Ltd. in Taiwan. I am currently a student at San Diego State University with a Graphic Design major and a minor in marketing and Honors minor in Interdisciplinary Studies graduating in 2025. My innovative mindset stems from my experience in working with different collaborative and professional environments and I continuously look for spaces to expand my skill set and utilize my unique approach to collage research and visual design.
